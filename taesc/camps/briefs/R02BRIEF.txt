Mission R02: Force the Strait

The enemy has fortified the narrow passage into Groshen Bay.  We need your Hulk transport ship and the Advanced Construction Vehicles it carries to further our war against the Core.

Move your troop transport containing the Advanced Construction Vehicles east through Groshen Strait.  You have twenty minutes to accomplish your mission.

Suggestions:
You must coordinate the various forces in order to be successful.  For example, use your warships to destroy the anti-aircraft guns, then attack with your Brawler gunships.

Battle Tactics:
Press CTRL+P to select all aircraft with weapons and CTRL+N for all naval units with weapons. CTRL+T will allow you to select all of your transport units.
